Privacy Policy

Introduction

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use "A Simple Way to Pray" (the "Service").

Information We Collect

Account Information: If you choose to sign in, we collect your name, email address, and profile picture from your chosen identity provider (Google). This is used to create your user profile and personalize your experience.

Phone Number: If you choose to enable SMS reminders, we collect your phone number to send you the requested notifications.

Usage Data: We use Google Analytics to collect anonymous usage data, such as page visits and device type, to improve the Service. This helps us understand how the website is used without personally identifying individual visitors.

User Content:

  • Prayer Requests: Any prayer requests you submit to the "Prayer Wall" are public and can be viewed by anyone. Please do not include sensitive personal information in these requests.
  • Personal Prayers: Prayers added to "My Personal Prayers" are private and encrypted. They are only visible to you when you are logged in.

Cookies

We use cookies to maintain your session when you are logged in and to remember your preferences (such as dark mode settings). We also use a cookie to anonymously track unique visitors for analytics purposes.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and personalize the Service.
  • Manage your account and preferences.
  • Facilitate the "Prayer Wall" and "My Personal Prayers" features.
  • Send prayer reminders via push notifications or SMS (if enabled).
  • Analyze usage to improve the website.

Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties, except as described below:

  • Identity Providers: We use Google OAuth for authentication.
  • SMS Service Providers: If you enable SMS reminders, your phone number will be processed by our SMS provider (Twilio) to deliver messages.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law.

Data Deletion

You may request the deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us via the Feedback form.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
