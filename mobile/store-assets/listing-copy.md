## App name (30 chars max)

A Simple Way to Pray

## Short description (80 chars max)

Daily prayer, scripture, and devotions in the Lutheran tradition.

## Full description (4000 chars max)

Inspired by Martin Luther's letter "A Simple Way to Pray," this app brings
the historic rhythm of daily prayer to your pocket — morning to night,
season by season.

PRAY THE DAILY OFFICES
Morning, Midday, Evening, Close of Day, and Night Watch devotions, each
with psalms, scripture readings, and prayers drawn from the historic
liturgy.

FOLLOW THE CHURCH YEAR
Menus, readings, and prayers change with the liturgical calendar — Advent
and Lenten devotions, feast days, and seasonal prayers appear as the
seasons turn.

READ THE WORD
• Daily lectionary readings
• Bible in a Year reading plan with progress tracking and streaks
• Psalms and Gospels by category
• Scripture memorization

LEARN THE FAITH
• Luther's Small Catechism, with a learning mode
• Nicene Creed and Trinity studies
• Children's and mid-week devotions
• The Litany

MAKE IT PERSONAL
• The Prayer Weaver: braid your own prayer from Luther's four strands —
  instruction, thanksgiving, confession, and petition
• Keep personal prayers private (encrypted) and organized by category
• Share requests on the community prayer wall and pray for others
• Track your prayer streak, with grace days built in

STAY IN RHYTHM
• Prayer reminders at the times you choose
• Download upcoming devotions for offline use
• Print-friendly pages for handouts and group study
• Dark mode and adjustable text size

Scripture quotations are from the ESV® Bible. Rooted in the tradition of
the Lutheran Church—Missouri Synod, and a blessing to any Christian
seeking a steady habit of prayer.

Free to use. Sign in to keep your prayers, streaks, and reminders in sync
across your devices.
